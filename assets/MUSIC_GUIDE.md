# Add a Classy Malayalam Wedding Song

The site is wired to play a song on tap from the bottom-right music button.

## Step 1 — Pick a song

Recommended classy & elegant Malayalam wedding songs (use an instrumental version where possible for a sophisticated invite feel):

### Soft & Romantic (great for invitations)
- **Pavizhamalli Poo Pole** — *Kanmadam* (Kaithapram, Raveendran)
- **Etho Janma Kalpanayil** — *Palunku*
- **Pranayame** — *Bangalore Days*
- **Mukile Mukile** — *Kireedam*
- **Aaro Padunnu Doore** — *Aniyathipraavu*

### Traditional & Classical (most elegant)
- **Devasabhaathalam** — Sopanam style traditional
- **Nadaswaram Wedding Instrumental** — Kerala traditional
- Any classical **Sopanam** or **Panchavadyam** clip
- **Bhagyada Lakshmi Baramma** — Carnatic classic, often used at weddings

### Modern Soulful
- **Thaazhvarayil** — *Shyamambaram*
- **Thumbi Vaa** — *Olangal*
- **Kannile Kaarmukile** — *Pavithram*

## Step 2 — Get the audio file

Save the file as **`music.mp3`** in this folder (`assets/music.mp3`). It will then auto-play when the visitor taps the music icon on the page.

You can convert YouTube videos to MP3 using a tool of your choice (for personal use). For royalty-free options:
- https://pixabay.com/music/search/wedding/
- https://www.epidemicsound.com/
- https://incompetech.com/

## Step 3 — Reload the page

Refresh the browser. Tap the music icon at the bottom-right of the page to play.

> Tip: Keep the file under 5 MB and around 2–3 minutes of audio for a smooth experience.
